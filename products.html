Grease Guns, Suction Guns, and Transfer Pumps

Suction Industrial Co., Ltd. specializes in manufacturing lubrication equipment made of cast iron, zinc alloys, and aluminum alloys. Our products include manual and air-powered greasing tools and accessories, suction guns, and pumps, and are also available in tool kits.

Suction’s grease guns, suction guns, and pumps are durable and of high quality; they meet the mechanic’s requirement of having a long-lasting and easy-to-operate tool to make lubricating efficient. They are easy-to-use and designed to minimize the issues of air blockage and lubricant bypass. Suction’s products are easy to clean and maintain.

Suction’s grease guns include patented grease guns. They are available in different functional designs and excellent for farm/agricultural, industrial, construction, and private/DIY applications. All grease guns featured a knurled body for a firm and safe grip. They also feature a durable follower that ensures positive priming and uninterrupted pumping action and precision fit and hardened plunger that prevents lubricant bypass and is scoring-resistant for longer life. Our grease guns include the most common air-operated grease guns, lever grease guns, and pistol-grip grease guns. We also produce special types of grease guns such as Jiabara (Japanese) type grease guns and push-type grease guns.

Besides grease guns, Suction manufactures suction guns of different volumes for drawing up oil, transfer pumps for fulfilling various needs in transferring oil and lubricant and various grease gun accessories such as spouts, nozzles, grease fittings, etc.
